VROOM!! has a dedicated staff that recruits, trains, and tends to our herd of BEASTS. We take great pride in the quality of Cars and rydes that we provide to our customers, and our staff exercises the utmost care in vetting the Beasts that join our herd.

Every JDM beast goes through a rigorous inspection process where we perform background checks, performance diagnostics, and several rounds of test drives. Cars accepted into the Vroom garage are then treated to the best tuning, detailing, and maintenance possible. We provide them with top-tier parts, premium fuel, and constant care. This is part of our garage philosophy: when the ride runs smooth and clean, the driver rides happy.
